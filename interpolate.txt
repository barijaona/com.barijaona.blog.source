Test on interpolate_fancy
meta-markup: none
<p>This is a test of the optional ability of <i>interpolate_fancy</i> to interpolate into stories (behaviour enabled by setting the variable $Blosxom::Plugin::Interpolate_fancy::recurse_into_story to 1)</p>

<p>This is not interpreted : $path  ,   $url</p>


<p>This is interpolated : <$path /><$url />/filename</p>

<p>Your time zone is <$utc_offset />.</p>

<p>The following bit of text is devoid of HTML:
<@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.strip_html output="Yes">
Silly <a href="http://www.raelity.org/">me</a>, I plumb <em>forgot</em> to remove the HTML.
</@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.strip_html></p>

<p>The following bit of text is only 20 characters in length:
<@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.foreshorten length="20" output="Yes">This text is far longer than 20 characters on the page, but will only appear as "This text is far lon" in the resulting page.
</@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.foreshorten></p>

<p>And in combination, stripping the HTML _before_ foreshortening : 
the strip_html action is embedded inside the foreshorten action and
thus is run first. The following bit of text is only 20 characters in length and devoid of HTML: <@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.foreshorten length="20" output="Yes"><@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.strip_html output="Yes">Silly <a href="http://www.raelity.org/">me</a>, I plumb <em>forgot</em> to remove the HTML.
</@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.strip_html>
</@Blosxom::Plugin::MyTest.foreshorten>
</p>
